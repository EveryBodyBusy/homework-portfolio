Jeremiah Chiu

About Portfolio

Jeremiah Chiu is an artist, musician, and graphic designer. He received his MFA in design from the University of Illinois at Chicago, focusing on sound visualizations through field recordings and interactive installations. In 2008, he co-founded the award-winning graphic design studio, Plural, where his personal practice as an artist and musician continues to influence and transcend boundaries between art, design, and music. More recently, his piece, Smartphone Symphony, an audio visual chance music performance, was performed at the Museum of Contemporary Art Chicago as part of the MCA PrimeTime series and on December 5, his Visual Music Collaboration with Butchy Fuego premiered at The Getty.
